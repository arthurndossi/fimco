Lawrence Mafuru

Chairman at FIMCO

BIOGRAPHY

Lawrence is a seasoned Certified Chartered Banker from the Chartered Institute of Bankers London and a Certified Financial Markets Dealer by ACI Financial Markets. He holds an Executive MBA Finance from University of Dar es Salaam Business school. His varied experience in the industry over the last 19 years includes roles as Head of Treasury (NBC), CEO (NBC) and Chairman of the Tanzanian Bankers Association. Lawrence has also worked on the public side extensively as the Treasury Registrar at the ministry of finance and also for the Presidential Delivery Bureau as Director of resource mobilisation. A noticeable figure and sought after speaker on matters related to banking and finance, public and private, Lawrence brings to Bankable his vast experience and expertise to achieving our mission and vision. Lawrence is passionate about driving client value through shared insights.
