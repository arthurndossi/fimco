What does FIMCO offer?

We offer clients access to market prices as they develop throughout the trading across Forex, Stocks, interest rates and Macro data.

Access to market information includes historical data and is a powerful in the hands of investors to drive their investment decisions. We offer a free limited view of market information as well as fee based service for individuals and corporates that covers more data and more historical analysis.

Access our platform by registering for a POCHI account and choosing market information.
