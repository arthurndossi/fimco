Fund Management

We offer our clients access to different investment opportunities that attempt to maximize their returns and manage risks accordingly.

Client can choose from standardized fund solutions such as POCHI, or our Dollar Fund to maximize on their returns by pooling funds by FIMCO which then invests diligently in the local markets; or bespoke solutions that cater to our clients Size of investment, liquidity preference, risk adverseness and the yields that the market has to offer.

FIMCO aims to invest on behalf of our clients — from government, large institutions to foundations, medium and small companies, parents and grandparents, teachers, nurses, doctors and people from all walks of life.

We are client focussed and our responsibility is to ensure that our clients get the insight into how to invest their by providing solutions that can help them build a better financial future.

Our background speaks by itself – being passionate about we do and intensely focused on performing at the highest levels. The challenges in our market are highly complex and as fund managers we strive to out-think to what will be the best balance of risk and return across all available investment opportunities on behalf of our clients.

Our clients’ investment objectives comes first and it is our responsibility to ensure that we meet both your short-term and long-term goals.

Our process

  • Registration (Basic KYC) Fill a form or two so that we know who you really are!
  • Execute Investment Mandate Legal protection from any liability; good for us and great for you
  • Initiate Investment transaction Tell us how much you’re looking to invest and we shall tell you how!
  • Fund transfer Transfer cash to our investment trust account and let us handle the rest
  • Investment execution We invest and you receive notification of confirmation.
