About Us

FIMCO is a Financial Investment Management Company that seeks offer professional assistance to clients looking to navigate Tanzanian ﬁnancial markets across various asset classes.

After working in commercial banks for over 25 years combined FIMCO founders and directors have been driven with the need to spread financial market literacy and capabilities by informing, advising and executing investments with and for clients.

This assistance through offering relevant information, advisory or execution capabilities is underpinned by the deep understanding of local market dynamics and core values committed to Diligence, Integrity and Innovation in all we do. We are looking to introduce local and foreign investors to the Tanzanian Financial markets opportunities, and indeed vice versa. We do so leveraging years of experience working with international banks in Tanzanian and a rich track record of engaging with clients and regulators alike. Whether it’s to sell or hold; we work hard to give our clients sufficient confidence to make this decision across various asset classes.

OUR INVESTMENT APPROACH

At FIMCO, we know our clients’ anxiety of local financial markets is more than it should be, as their knowledge of the market and opportunities is generally less than it should be. Our investment approach targets to firmly bridge this gap.

From the first interaction until maturity; we aim to adequately inform you of the key matters for your investments, advise you of the risk adjusted opportunities possible to you and execute on opportunities as efficiently as possible.

This will be demonstrated throughout simple on boarding process, the accessibility of resources to assist you and the simplified execution process.
