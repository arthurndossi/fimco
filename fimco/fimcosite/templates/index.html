Welcome to FIMCO

{#

"HABA na HABA HUJAZA KIBABA..."

#}

"Little by little, a little becomes a lot"; "Haba na haba hujaza kibaba"

Anonymous

Financial Investment Management Company dedicated to assisting our clients to invest more transparently, competitively and affordably.

This assistance we offer via relevant information, advisory and execution capabilities is underpinned by the deep understanding of local market dynamics and commitment to our core values of Diligence, Integrity and Innovation in all we do.

We are looking to introduce local and foreign investors to the Tanzanian Financial markets opportunities, and indeed vice versa. We do so leveraging years of experience working with international banks in Tanzanian and a rich track record of engaging with clients and regulators alike.

Whether it’s to buy, sell or hold, whether it’s to invest, disinvest or hold; we work hard to give our clients sufficient confidence to make this decision across various asset classes in our financial markets in a way that creates lasting long term value for them.
