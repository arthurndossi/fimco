Brokerage

As Licensed Dealing Members (LDM) we offer our clients access to equities listed on the Dar es salaam (DSE), Nairobi Stock Exchange (NSE) and Uganda Securities Exchange (USE).

Our approach to offering brokerage services is simple; we invest our expertise and values to create a platform that will allow clients to access the market transparently, competitively and efficiently. We give our client an intraday view of trading activities which adds more color and context to the closing or opening reports.

We are also a key focal point to navigating the East African (Kenya, Tanzania and Uganda) Fixed Income markets space. Using our extensive knowledge and networks of our Fixed Income markets we drive price discovery for local and offshore players.

Our services in equities and fixed income extend to both the primary and secondary markets.

Contact us and let us discuss how we can help you navigate the opportunities for your portfolio.
