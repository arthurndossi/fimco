Brokerage

We offer access to Dar es Salaam Stock Exchange listed securities as a licensed broker/dealer of securities. As Licensed Dealing Members (LDM) we offer our clients access to equities listed on the Dar es salaam Stock Exchange

Our approach to offering brokerage services is simple; we invest our expertise and values to create a platform that will allow clients to access the market transparently, competitively and efficiently. We give our client an intraday view of trading activities which adds more color and context to the closing or opening reports.

Whilst most intermediaries and market watchers provide a snapshot of activity at the end of the day; through our FIMCO platform we offer our clients a way of staying close to the DSE activity as they move throughout the trading day.

Contact us and let us discuss how we can help you navigate the opportunities in the DSE for your portfolio.
